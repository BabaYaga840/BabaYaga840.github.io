Soumojit Bhattacharya

I am a student at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, currently pursuing a B.Tech. in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering. My passion lies in the field of robotics, and I am enthusiastic about exploring its various facets through research.

Currently, I am an intern at the CLVR (Cognitive Learning for Vision and Robotics) lab, KAIST, under the supervision of Professor Joseph J. Lim.

I also serve as a student researcher at the Autonomous Ground Vehicle lab, IIT Kharagpur, under the guidance of Professor Debashish Chakravarty. Furthermore, I am a member of the electronics subsystem of the student satellite program at IIT Kharagpur.

Previously, I had the opportunity to work as an intern at IIT Bombay, supervised by Professor Arpita Sinha.

I am especially interested in reinforcement learning and its applications in robotics.
